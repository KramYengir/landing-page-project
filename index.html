BUKOWSKI

Goes Nowhere Goes Somewhere Goes Anywhere

It's all a joke, pal!

People browse these things called 'websites', they can do it hours at a time, a whole bunch of us, staring at these digtal pages of text and images, hoping to find some peace or fun or whatever, something, anything to distract us from all the madness...

Would ya look at this title! Man, what a piece of work...

My poetry was very un-poetry-like for the time, I just wrote what I thought about, good or bad, right or wrong...

I would sit at my clanky old typewriter and bang away at it into dawn, fueld by cigarettes and alcohol and some music by long-dead men...

I was a handsome sunuvah-bitch...

I did at all my way, it was messy and sore, lonely and depraved, but it was me and it was one hell of a ride...
